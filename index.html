What's iReporter All About?

Corruption is a major challenge in Africa.
Many solutions have been proposed for this aching
problem but it lingers in many African countries.
iReporter aims to do it's part in curbing this menace by
the use of technology.
With iReporter, anyone can report incidents of corrution.
Whether you are on the move or at home, you can take a few moments
report such incidents around your vicinity.
Also, alert the general public to uncompleted projects by taking snapshots
of such locations. Our services will try and locate you and provide relevant information.

Don't want your identity broadcasted? Well, we got you covered too.
You can instantly make an anonymous post and we'll be there to verify and broadcast.
So, got a smartphone? Whip it out and let's fight corruption together.
Let's build the Africa of our dreams. One post at a time!
