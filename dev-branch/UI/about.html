What's iReporter All About?

Corruption is a major challenge in Africa.
Many solutions have been proposed for this aching problem but it lingers in many African countries. iReporter aims to do it's part in curbing this menace by the use of technology.

With iReporter, anyone can report incidents of corrution. Whether you are on the move or at home, you can take a few moments report such incidents around your vicinity.

Also, alert the general public to uncompleted projects by taking snapshots of such locations. Our services will try and locate you and provide relevant information.
